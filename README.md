🌐 PACO Connects – About the Project

PACO Connects is a digital platform built to connect music producers and artists in Malawi through a fair, structured, and professional system.

In Malawi’s music industry, many collaborations happen informally. Producers often create high-quality beats but struggle to reach artists, receive proper credit, or earn consistent income. At the same time, artists find it difficult to access beats with clear licensing and legal protection.

PACO Connects solves this problem.

🎧 What PACO Connects Does

PACO Connects provides a centralized marketplace where:

Producers can upload and sell their beats
Artists can discover, preview, and purchase beats بسهولة
Every transaction includes clear and legally structured agreements
Payments are secure and accessible through mobile money and bank systems
💰 Fair Payment System

PACO Connects uses a simple and transparent 70/20/10 model:

70% goes directly to the producer
20% supports the platform and its growth
10% aligns with Copyright Society of Malawi for copyright support

Producers also continue to receive royalties when their music is played on radio, streaming platforms, or at public events, as protected under Malawi law.

⚖️ Built on Trust and Protection

Every beat uploaded to PACO Connects must:

Be 100% original
Follow strict copyright guidelines
Include proper licensing terms

The platform includes:

Identity verification for users
Secure payment processing
Clear ownership and usage agreements

This ensures fair and safe transactions for both producers and artists.

📚 Education & Growth

Beyond the platform, PACO Connects supports the creative community by:

Offering training on copyright and licensing
Providing guidance for new producers and artists
Promoting professional standards in the industry
🚀 Our Vision

PACO Connects is more than a marketplace.

It is a long-term solution designed to build a fair, structured, and profitable music industry in Malawi, where creators are respected, protected, and properly paid for their work.
