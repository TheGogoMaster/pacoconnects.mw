How it works

Registration

PACO Connects is home to some of the highest quality commercial music content around. It's also safe. Our diligent background checks help maintain our trusted status.

Identification

To get verified on our platform, every seller has to provide social media links and a government-issued photo ID. This information is encrypted, securely stored, and governed by our privacy policy.

At this stage, it's also good for a beatmaker to provide us with Facebook, Soundcloud, Instagram, and Spotify links, so we can better understand their production style.

We value producers with a significant release history and some commercial success. However, if you don't have any of that, it doesn't mean you will be rejected. Our main selection criteria are full transparency about the beatmaker's professional background and, of course, top quality beats.

Approval

Once you've submitted an application, your user status changes to Pending. What follows is the review process, which takes up to 3 days, after which we will notify you via email to let you know whether we have approved or declined your application.

We do not work with managers, agents, or agencies. We only work directly with beatmakers.

Music Requirement

Before submitting, please make sure that you do not violate these rules.

It's your beat

You produced this beat by yourself, and you own 100% of the rights. It doesn't belong to anyone else, it's not a collaboration beat, and you're not uploading it on anyone else's behalf.

The beat must be well produced, professionally mixed and mastered, and feature creative sound design.

It is forbidden to upload this beat online. The only exception is a short preview (15-30 seconds).

Package Requirement

Master

Mastered 44.1kHz 16-bit WAV and mastered 320kbps MP3.

Unmastered version

Unmastered 44.1kHz 16-bit WAV. If your beat has a vocal, include an unmastered instrumental WAV.

Stems

Unmastered multitrack 44.1kHz 16-bit WAV stems. Minimum 5 stems, correctly named.

Instrumental

Required only if the beat contains vocals.

Use of Samples

We prohibit the unaltered use of readymade melodic loops, melodic samples, presets, and MIDI files.

If royalty-free samples are used, you must specify the source. If you select exclusive samples, it means you created/recorded the main sounds yourself.

Sales & 70/20/10 Split

Our payout rate is 70% for the producer, 20% for PACO Connects, and 10% for COSOMA.

Withdrawal available to Mobile money and Bank accounts. Maximum payout period is 24 hours.

New beatmakers can set prices between MK50,000 and MK60,000. Maximum price increases with sales up to MK500,000.
